InvestConnect Website Planning Document

A comprehensive plan for creating the InvestConnect investment platform website

Site Name

InvestConnect (www.investconnect.mg)

This name was chosen because it effectively communicates the core purpose of the platform: connecting investors with investment opportunities. The name is straightforward, memorable, and clearly indicates the site's function as a connecting hub for investment activities. The ".mg" domain signifies the site's focus on the Madagascar market, providing a localized investment platform.

Site Purpose

InvestConnect serves as a digital platform connecting entrepreneurs and project owners with potential investors. The site's primary purpose is to:

  • Provide a showcase for entrepreneurs to present their business ideas and projects
  • Create a secure environment for investors to discover and fund promising ventures
  • Facilitate communication between project owners and investors
  • Educate visitors about investment opportunities in various sectors
  • Offer tools for project funding simulation and progress tracking

The platform aims to democratize access to funding for entrepreneurs while providing investors with a diverse portfolio of investment opportunities, ultimately contributing to economic development through collective funding initiatives.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: Entrepreneur Seeking Funding

"I have a promising business idea but lack the capital to get started. How can InvestConnect help me secure funding and what information do I need to provide to attract potential investors?"

Scenario 2: Potential Investor

"I'm interested in investing in startups but don't know where to find legitimate opportunities. How can I evaluate projects on InvestConnect and what security measures are in place to protect my investment?"

Scenario 3: First-time Visitor

"I've heard about crowdfunding but never participated before. What are the basic steps to get involved either as an investor or project owner on InvestConnect?"

Color Schema

The color scheme is designed to convey trust, professionalism, and innovation, which are essential qualities for a financial platform:

Primary Color: #005f73 (Deep Teal)

Usage: Main headers, navigation bar, footer, and primary UI elements. This color conveys trust and stability.

Secondary Color: #ee9b00 (Golden Orange)

Usage: Call-to-action buttons, highlights, progress bars, and interactive elements to draw attention.

Background Color: #f8f9fa (Light Gray)

Usage: Page backgrounds and content containers for a clean, professional look.

Text Color: #333333 (Dark Gray)

Usage: Body text for optimal readability against light backgrounds.

Accent Color: #94d2bd (Light Teal)

Usage: Borders, separators, and subtle UI elements to complement the primary color.

Typography

The website will primarily use the 'Segoe UI' font family with fallbacks for cross-platform compatibility:

Heading 1

Usage: Main page titles and major section headers (24-32px, bold, primary color)

Heading 2

Usage: Section headers and subheadings (20-24px, semi-bold, primary color)

Heading 3

Usage: Minor section headers and form labels (18px, medium weight, primary color)

Body Text

Usage: Main content text (16px, regular weight, text color)

Small Text

Usage: Footer text, disclaimers, and additional information (14px, regular weight, text color)

Usage: Call-to-action buttons and interactive elements (16px, bold, white color on secondary background)
